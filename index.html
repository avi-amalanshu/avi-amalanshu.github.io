I am pursuing a dual-degree (Bachelor and Master of Technology) from the Department of Electronics & Electrical Communication Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. I plan to specialize in Vision and Intelligent Systems for my Masters (full 5 year curriculum here). I also plan to complete a minor degree in Computer Science & Engineering. I am a member of the Autonomous Ground Vehicle (AGV) research group here at IIT Kharagpur where I use deep learning on problems of robotic perception and path planning. In particular, I work on human and vehicle trajectory prediction.
My research interests span AI and Systems Design and their interdisciplinary applications, particularly in autonomous and biological/cognitive/social systems. My goal is to contribute to cutting-edge work that helps us understand signals in ourselves and our surroundings, and improve the way we interact with them.
