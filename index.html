Research

My research interests as an undergrad are broad and growing broader as I learn and am exposed to new topics. My long-term goal can be characterized as methodology for intelligent systems to make faster and safer decisions (especially in multi-agent environments). This has implications in high-output fields like robotics, data analytics, finance, comms networks, and biomedical engg. I draw inspiration from many fascinating areas like ML, optimization, game theory, information theory, control theory, distributed systems, signal processing, cryptography etc.
